# Privacy Policy — Killer Sudoku by Seventh Chronicle

**Last updated:** 5 April 2026

## Overview

Killer Sudoku ("the App") is developed by Ben Larson, trading as Seventh Chronicle ("I", "me"). This privacy policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data the App Does NOT Collect

The App does not collect, request, or store:
- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your location
- Photos, files, or media
- Health, financial, or biometric data
- Contacts or call logs
- Browsing history

No account or login is required to use the App.

## Anonymous Usage Analytics

The App collects anonymous usage data through the LiteTrack analytics service to help improve the app experience. This includes:
- Which screens are viewed
- Gameplay events (game started, completed, difficulty chosen, hints used)
- Feature usage (streaks, daily puzzles, settings changes)
- Purchase flow events (sheet viewed, purchase completed or cancelled — no financial details)
- Ad impression counts

This data contains **no personal identifiers** — no names, emails, IP addresses, or device identifiers are collected by the analytics service.

**You can opt out at any time** in the App's Settings screen under "Share anonymous analytics". When disabled, no analytics data is sent.

## Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob (interstitial and rewarded ads). Google's advertising SDK may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and use them to serve personalised or non-personalised ads in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Premium users who purchase the ad-free upgrade will no longer see ads and the AdMob SDK will not serve ad requests.

## In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time premium purchase processed entirely by Google Play. I do not have access to your payment details. Google's handling of purchase data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Local Data

All game data — saved games, statistics, preferences, streak records, and puzzle progress — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database and is not transmitted to any server. Uninstalling the App deletes this data.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:
- **Google AdMob** (advertising): [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Play Billing** (in-app purchases): [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **LiteTrack** (anonymous analytics): data is transmitted to a secure endpoint over HTTPS

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 16. I do not knowingly collect any personal data from children or anyone else.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
